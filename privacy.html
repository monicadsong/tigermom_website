Privacy Policy

Usage Data

As is standard with Android applications distributed through the Google Play Store, Google gathers and shares certain non-personally identifiable information (such as usage statistics) with the particular application developer using built-in functionality of the Google Play Store. We also receive this non-personally identifiable data, as well as further similar anonymous data gathered on the users device such as daily phone usage times and top five apps used and their times.

We use this data for business operations and planning, including application improvement, debugging, and marketing.

For the purposes of our social function, we track and store the user's name, email, and weekly usage data. This is stored securely and not shared with any third parties. Although this is done to fulfill the function of sharing data with the user's added friends, the data is sent to and stored on our server even if the user adds no friends.

Data Stored on your Device

One of Tigermom's main functions is to monitor the user's phone usage to display the data to them and their friends. This requires the gathering and storing of phone usage data on your device.This data includes total time spent on phone, as well as time spent on each app.

We do not store any personal or sensitive information such as phone numbers, passwords, photos, etc.

Camera

Tigermom requires camera access. This is purely for scanning QR codes to add friends, and used for no other reasons. At no point does Tigermom obtain any other information through your camera.
